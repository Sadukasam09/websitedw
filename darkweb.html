Welcome to Dark Web

What is the Dark Web?

The Dark Web is a part of the internet that isn't indexed by search engines. You've no doubt heard talk of the “dark web” as a hotbed of criminal activity.

How to Access the Dark Web?

Accessing the Dark Web requires the use of an anonymizing browser called Tor. The Tor browser routes your web page requests through a series of proxy servers operated by thousands of volunteers around the globe, rendering your IP address unidentifiable and untraceable.

Is it safe to browse the Dark Web?

While the Dark Web is often associated with illegal activities, there are legitimate reasons to use the dark web. Remember, though, to be cautious and use common sense over there.

What can you find on the Dark Web?

There are a lot of things you can find on the Dark Web, including:

  • Illegal drugs
  • Weapons
  • Counterfeit money
  • Stolen credit card numbers
  • Guides on how to commit various crimes
  • And much more
