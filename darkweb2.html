Dark Web

DARK WEB vs DEEP WEB

The Deep Web refers to all parts of the internet that cannot be indexed by traditional search engines, such as email accounts, banking details, and subscription-only information. This includes both innocuous content and the more nefarious Dark Web.

The Dark Web, on the other hand, is a small portion of the Deep Web that has been intentionally hidden and is inaccessible through standard web browsers. The Dark Web is often associated with a marketplace for drugs, firearms, hacking tools, and more illicit goods and services.
